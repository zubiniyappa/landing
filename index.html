About Us

Conscious Nomad is an online aggregator that specializes in sustainable travel in the form of both a mobile application and a website to book hotels and restaurants.

Learn More

Our Services

  • For travellers, we provide an aggregator portal (website, app) to make sustainable holiday and leisure decisions. Whether it be through our range of eco-conscious vetted hotels, restaurants or good for the environment experiences!
  • For businesses looking to list on Conscious Nomad, we do more than just give their offerings a platform through our travel portal. We also offer a consultancy service to help our clients audit, improve and implement sustainable hospitality best practices.
